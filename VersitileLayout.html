About This Map

Washington, DC, the capital of the United States, is home to the nation's vital governmental systems. It is also home to several renown tourist attractions from theaters to parks.

This map shows just a handful of the many fun places around DC to explore. Click on each symbol on the map to learn more! You can also filter based on the type of attraction using the blue guide in the top right corner of the map.
